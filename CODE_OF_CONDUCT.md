# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery outside of clearly-scoped NSFW content packs
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without their explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Note on Pack Content

Some spinner packs in this repository (tagged `nsfw`) contain vulgar language drawn from their source material — Czech/Slovak internet culture often includes crude humor. That content is opt-in and namespace-isolated.

**Crude language quoting source material is allowed; harassment or slurs directed at people, individually or as groups, is not.** Contributors should apply this distinction when submitting new packs.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers via GitHub Issues marked "conduct" or directly to the project owner listed in the repo's `.claude-plugin/plugin.json`.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
